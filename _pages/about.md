## Biography

I am currently a **Lecturer** at the School of Computer Science, **Southwest Petroleum University (SWPU)**. 

I earned my Ph.D. in Electronic Information from the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the **University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)**, where I was a member of the Internet Security Team supervised by [**Prof. Hongbo Liu**](https://www.scse.uestc.edu.cn/info/1081/11244.htm). Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed my **Master's degree in Cybersecurity** at UESTC under the supervision of [**Prof. Chunxiang Xu**](https://www.scse.uestc.edu.cn/info/1081/11924.htm) within the Applied Cryptography Team, focusing on blockchain security and privacy preservation.

From September 2024 to September 2025, I served as a visiting Ph.D. scholar at the Faculty of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering, **University of Oulu**, Finland, working with [**Prof. Shuai Li**](https://www.oulu.fi/en/researchers/shuai-li), under the support of the China Scholarship Council (CSC).

My research focuses on **Physical Layer Security** and **Wireless Secret Key Generation**. To date, I have authored 19 academic papers, including 4 first-author publications in top-tier journals such as *IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing* (CCF-A), and I hold 3 authorized invention patents.

---

## Research Interests

* **Physical Layer Security:** Enhancing wireless network security through physical layer characteristics.
* **Secret Key Generation:** Developing practical key extraction protocols for wireless environments.
* **Blockchain Security:** Investigating fairness, privacy-preservation, and auditability in decentralized transaction systems.
