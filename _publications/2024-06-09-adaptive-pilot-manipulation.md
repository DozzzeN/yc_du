### Contributions & Highlights
* **Active Channel Probing:** Developed a pioneering **adaptive pilot manipulation** strategy that moves beyond passive observation, allowing for the proactive shaping of desired channel measurement patterns.
* **Pattern Distinctiveness:** Successfully enhanced the similarity between matched segments while simultaneously ensuring the uniqueness of each pattern, which is critical for preventing collisions in the secret key space.
* **Environmental Robustness:** Significantly improved key generation reliability and accuracy in high-noise environments, providing a robust security layer for dynamic wireless communication systems.
