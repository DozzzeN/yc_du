### Contributions & Highlights
* **First Physical Attack on WiFi Sensing:** Engineered the first practical physical adversarial attack targeting DL-based WiFi sensing systems by manipulating WiFi preambles to inject stealthy perturbations into CSI.
* **Quantitative CSI Control:** Developed a method to quantitatively manipulate receiver-side CSI by modifying pilot signals at the transmitter, ensuring the attack remains unnoticeable to normal communication protocols.
* **Universal Perturbation Optimization:** Proposed an optimization framework based on **C&W attacks** to ensure attack effectiveness under various CSI responses and environmental noise, achieving high success rates against activity recognition and user authentication systems.
