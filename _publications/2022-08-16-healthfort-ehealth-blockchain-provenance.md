### Contributions & Highlights
* **Conditional Forward Transparency:** Developed a server-aided, password-based **subsequent-key-locked encryption** mechanism. This ensures that doctors can only access Electronic Health Records (EHRs) specifically delegated for the current diagnosis, preventing unauthorized access to historical or future sensitive data.
* **Portable & Secret-Free Delegation:** Engineered a highly portable delegation protocol where patients can authorize access by sending a single **256-bit key**. This eliminates the need for patients to maintain complex secrets or cryptographic materials on their local mobile devices, enhancing usability for eHealth applications.
* **Blockchain-based Secure Provenance:** Integrated a decentralized provenance mechanism using **smart contracts** on a public blockchain. By designing a specialized EHR provenance data structure, the system provides a tamper-proof and precisely auditable record of data ownership and access history throughout the EHR lifecycle.
