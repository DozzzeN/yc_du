### Contributions & Highlights
* **Adaptive Segmentation:** Addressed the critical trade-off between key generation rate (KGR) and accuracy caused by fixed-length segmentation in traditional matching-based methods.
* **DTW-based Partitioning:** Proposed a framework that dynamically partitions channel samples based on **Dynamic Time Warping (DTW)** distance to maximize inter-segment distinctiveness.
* **Recursive Retrieval:** Developed a **recursive rapid retrieval mechanism** to efficiently identify consistent patterns, significantly outperforming state-of-the-art methods in robustness and efficiency.
