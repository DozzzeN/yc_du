### Contributions & Highlights
* **Novel Side-Channel:** Revealed a severe threat where PINs are inferred from arm/elbow posture changes captured via video from behind.
* **Posture Estimation:** Developed a mechanism to track upper-limb trajectories and detect keystrokes via elbow angle variations and fingertip position estimation.
