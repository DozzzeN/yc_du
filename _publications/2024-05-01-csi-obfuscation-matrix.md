### Contributions & Highlights
* **Carrier-based Transmission:** Tackled the channel non-reciprocity bottleneck by shifting from direct entropy extraction to a "key encapsulation" model using channel measurements.
* **CSI Obfuscation:** Proposed a **cyclic convolution-based CSI obfuscation** technique for secure key encapsulation.
* **Reliable Extraction:** Leveraged **quadratic programming and matrix equalization** for rapid key extraction, effectively eliminating bit mismatches caused by hardware non-reciprocity.
