### Contributions & Highlights
* **Index-based Matching:** Introduced **SIM-SKG**, a novel paradigm that utilizes the **sorted indices** of channel measurements rather than raw channel samples to achieve error-free key generation.
* **Anti-interference:** Designed a **noise perturbation scheme** to resolve index ambiguity caused by half-duplex mode and ambient noise.
* **Performance:** Capable of generating a **2048-bit error-free key within only 1.7 ms**, demonstrating superior resistance against various eavesdropping attacks.
