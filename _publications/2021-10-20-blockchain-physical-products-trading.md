### Contributions & Highlights
* **Untrusted Logistics Modeling:** Identified and addressed a critical gap in existing e-commerce systems by treating the **express/logistics entity** as a rational, potentially untrusted actor. This model prevents delivery services from compromising user privacy or breaking transaction security for profit.
* **Decentralized PBOT Framework:** Developed **PBOT**, a blockchain-based architecture that eliminates the "single-point-of-failure" inherent in centralized designs. The system provides a robust environment for physical product trading, integrating **fairness, privacy preservation, and auditability** into a unified protocol.
* **Security & Performance Validation:** Conducted a comprehensive security analysis and performance evaluation of core modules. The results demonstrate that PBOT is not only secure against various rational attacks but also highly feasible and efficient for real-world smart city applications.
