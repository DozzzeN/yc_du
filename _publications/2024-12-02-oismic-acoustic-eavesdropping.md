### Contributions & Highlights
* **Novel Vulnerability Discovery:** Identified that external acoustic waves can trigger microscopic vibrations in smartphone **Optical Image Stabilization (OIS)** components, leading to observable electromagnetic (EM) leakage.
* **Zero-Permission Sensing:** Developed an attack pipeline that reconstructs audio by capturing EM leakage signals without requiring any system-level permissions (e.g., microphone access), bypassing traditional security defenses.
* **Signal Reconstruction:** Utilized **Wavelet Transforms** and **Diffusion-based Neural Networks** to eliminate phase errors and restore high-fidelity speech information from noisy EM signals.
