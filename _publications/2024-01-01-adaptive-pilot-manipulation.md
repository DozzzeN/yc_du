### Contributions & Highlights
* **Active Channel Shaping:** Developed an **adaptive pilot manipulation** strategy to proactively shape the desired channel measurement patterns.
* **Robustness:** Enhanced the similarity between matched segments while ensuring uniqueness, significantly improving key generation robustness and accuracy in high-noise environments.
