### Contributions & Highlights
* **Dual-View Imaging Framework:** Proposed a privacy-preserving indoor perception system that captures dual-view human imaging using commodity WiFi devices. This multi-perspective approach effectively addresses the **body ambiguity** and occlusion issues prevalent in traditional single-view WiFi sensing, especially when multiple individuals overlap.
* **Stable Video Reconstruction:** Developed a deep-learning-based model to generate human images from WiFi Channel State Information (CSI). An innovative **anomaly detection algorithm** was integrated to identify and replace outlier frames with stable ones, ensuring smoother and more coherent human perception videos.
* **Multi-Person Scenario Validation:** Evaluated the system's performance in complex scenarios involving up to three individuals. Experimental results demonstrated high accuracy in Subject Overlap (SO) detection, achieving **94.4% for single-person** and maintaining robust performance even in high-density multi-person environments.
