### Contributions & Highlights
* **Index-based Matching:** Introduced **SIM-SKG**, a pioneering paradigm that utilizes the **sorted indices** of channel measurements instead of raw channel samples to achieve deterministic, error-free key generation.
* **Resilience to Interference:** Designed a robust **noise perturbation scheme** to resolve index ambiguity stemming from half-duplex communication constraints and ambient environmental noise.
* **Efficiency & Security:** Demonstrated the ability to generate a **2048-bit error-free key in just 1.7 ms**, while providing superior resistance against sophisticated eavesdropping attacks.
