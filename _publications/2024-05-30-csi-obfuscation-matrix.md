### Contributions & Highlights
* **Key Encapsulation Paradigm:** Addresses the channel non-reciprocity bottleneck by shifting from traditional direct entropy extraction to a novel **key encapsulation** model that utilizes channel measurements as secure carriers.
* **CSI Obfuscation Technique:** Proposes a robust **cyclic convolution-based CSI obfuscation** method to protect encapsulated keys against sophisticated eavesdropping.
* **Optimized Key Extraction:** Leverages **quadratic programming and matrix equalization** to achieve rapid and reliable key extraction, effectively eliminating bit mismatches inherent in hardware non-reciprocity.
