### Contributions & Highlights
* **Matching-based Paradigm:** Proposed a novel key generation framework that matches randomly manipulated channel measurements instead of traditional quantization, effectively resolving inconsistencies caused by asynchronous probing and noise.
* **Dual Alignment Algorithms:** Developed two sophisticated matching methods: **Bipartite Graph Matching** (leveraging measurement permutations) and **Edit-Sequence Alignment** (handling channel variations via sequence editing).
* **Efficiency & Entropy Enhancements:** Introduced a channel reuse mechanism and an optimized information reconciliation scheme, ensuring high-entropy, error-free key agreement with low computational overhead across diverse wireless environments.
