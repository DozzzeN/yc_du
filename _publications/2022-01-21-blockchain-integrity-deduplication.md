### Contributions & Highlights
* **Decentralized Auditing:** Developed a transparent integrity auditing framework that eliminates the need for trusted third-party auditors by leveraging blockchain for verifiable evidence storage.
* **Secure Deduplication:** Engineered a scheme supporting deduplication over encrypted data, allowing users to benefit from storage efficiency while maintaining content confidentiality against non-owners.
* **Accountability:** Integrated auditing and deduplication evidence into blockchain transactions, enabling users to verify cloud server compliance and ownership patterns autonomously.
